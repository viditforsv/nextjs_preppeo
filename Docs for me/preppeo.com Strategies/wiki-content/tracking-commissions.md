# Tracking Commissions

Every time a student purchases mock tokens using your partner code, you earn commission. Here's how tracking and payouts work.

---

## How Commission Is Calculated

- Commission rate: **30%** of the amount the student pays (after discount).
- The student discount is **10%** off the listed price.

**Example:**
- Student buys a 3-Mock pack (listed at ₹999).
- Your code gives 10% off → student pays ₹899.10.
- Your commission: 30% of ₹899.10 = **₹269.73**.

---

## Viewing Your Commissions

Currently, commission data is tracked on our end. Contact us at [contact@preppeo.com](mailto:contact@preppeo.com) to request a commission statement.

> **Coming soon:** A self-serve partner dashboard where you can view referral counts, commission history, and payout status in real time.

---

## Payout Process

1. Commissions are tallied monthly.
2. We contact you with a statement and process the payout.
3. Payouts are made via bank transfer.

Minimum payout threshold: ₹500.

---

## Commission Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Purchase completed, commission recorded, not yet paid |
| Paid | Commission transferred to your bank account |
