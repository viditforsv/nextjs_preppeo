# Partner Program: Getting Started

The Preppeo Partner Program lets teachers, tutoring centers, and coaching institutes earn commission by recommending Preppeo mocks to their students.

---

## How It Works

1. **Apply** — Contact us to join the partner program.
2. **Get your code** — We assign you a unique partner referral code (e.g., COACHING30).
3. **Share** — Your students use the code at checkout to get 10% off.
4. **Earn** — You earn 30% commission on every purchase made with your code.

---

## How to Apply

1. Go to [preppeo.com/for-institutes](https://preppeo.com/for-institutes).
2. Click **Become a Partner**.

![Become a Partner button](placeholder-become-partner.png)

3. Fill out the contact form. Select "Partnership" as the subject.

![Partnership contact form](placeholder-partnership-form.png)

4. Our team will review your application and set up your partner account within 1-2 business days.

---

## What You Receive

- A unique partner referral code
- 10% discount for your students (configurable)
- 30% commission on every student purchase (configurable)
- Access to institute bulk pricing for direct token distribution

---

## Commission Details

- Commission is calculated on the amount the student actually pays (after discount).
- Commissions are tracked automatically and paid out periodically.
- Example: Student buys a 3-pack for ₹999, your code gives 10% off (student pays ₹899), you earn 30% of ₹899 = ₹269.70.
