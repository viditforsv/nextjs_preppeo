# How to Start a Mock

This guide walks you through taking a full-length adaptive mock test on Preppeo.

---

## Step 1: Go to the Mock Hub

Click **Mocks** in the navigation bar, or go to [preppeo.com/mocks](https://preppeo.com/mocks).

![Mock Hub page](placeholder-mock-hub.png)

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Exam

Select the exam you want to take — SAT, GRE, Ashoka, or others.

![Exam selection cards](placeholder-exam-cards.png)

---

## Step 3: Enter Your Access Code

Each mock requires an access code (token). If you have a free complimentary token or purchased tokens, enter the code when prompted.

![Access code entry screen](placeholder-access-code.png)

- **Don't have a code?** Click "Claim Free Mock" to get your complimentary token, or visit the [Token Store](how-to-buy-mock-tokens.md) to purchase more.

---

## Step 4: Start the Mock

Once your code is verified, you'll see the mock configuration screen. Review the details and click **Start Mock**.

![Mock start screen](placeholder-mock-start.png)

- The test is timed — a countdown timer appears at the top.
- For the SAT, Module 2 adapts based on your Module 1 performance, just like the real exam.

---

## Step 5: Submit and View Results

After completing all sections, submit your test. Your score report generates immediately.

![Submit confirmation](placeholder-submit-mock.png)

---

## Tips

- Use a quiet environment and treat it like the real exam.
- Don't close the browser tab during the test — your progress is saved, but re-entry may cost time.
- Review your results carefully — see [Understanding Your Score Report](understanding-your-score-report.md).
