# Getting Started with Preppeo

Welcome to Preppeo — a platform for full-length adaptive mock tests for the Digital SAT, GRE, GMAT, and more.

---

## Create Your Account

1. Go to [preppeo.com](https://preppeo.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.

![Step 1: Click Sign Up](placeholder-signup-button.png)

2. Enter your name, email address, and create a password. Alternatively, sign up with Google for one-click access.

![Step 2: Fill in your details](placeholder-signup-form.png)

3. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account.

![Step 3: Verify your email](placeholder-email-verification.png)

---

## First Login

1. After verification, log in at [preppeo.com/auth](https://preppeo.com/auth).

2. You'll land on the homepage. From here you can:
   - **Start a free mock** — your first SAT mock is complimentary
   - **Explore the Mock Hub** — browse available exams
   - **Try Practice Mode** — 5 free questions per day

![Homepage after login](placeholder-homepage-logged-in.png)

---

## What's Included for Free

- 1 complimentary full-length SAT mock (Set 1)
- 5 free practice questions per day (2 easy + 2 medium + 1 hard)
- Detailed score report with domain breakdown after every mock
