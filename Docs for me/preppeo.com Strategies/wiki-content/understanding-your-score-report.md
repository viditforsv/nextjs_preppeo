# Understanding Your Score Report

After completing a mock, you get a detailed score report. Here's how to read it.

---

## Overall Score

Your composite score is displayed on the 400–1600 scale (for SAT). This is broken down into two section scores: Reading & Writing and Math.

![Overall score view](placeholder-score-overview.png)

---

## Domain Breakdown

Each section is further broken down by domain. For SAT Math, you'll see:
- Algebra
- Advanced Math
- Problem Solving & Data Analysis
- Geometry & Trigonometry

![Domain breakdown chart](placeholder-domain-breakdown.png)

This tells you exactly which areas need the most work.

---

## Difficulty Analysis

See how you performed across Easy, Medium, and Hard questions. If you're getting Easy questions wrong, focus on fundamentals. If you're missing Hard ones, it's an expected pattern for mid-range scores.

![Difficulty tier breakdown](placeholder-difficulty-breakdown.png)

---

## Question-Level Review

Click on any question to see:
- Your answer vs. the correct answer
- A detailed explanation
- The domain and difficulty tier

![Question review screen](placeholder-question-review.png)

---

## Score History

Track your progress over time at [preppeo.com/sat-test/history](https://preppeo.com/sat-test/history). Each mock's score is plotted so you can see your improvement trend.

![Score history graph](placeholder-score-history.png)
