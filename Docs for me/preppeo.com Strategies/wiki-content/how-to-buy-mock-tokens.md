# How to Buy Mock Tokens

Each full-length mock requires one token. Here's how to purchase them.

---

## Step 1: Go to the Token Store

Navigate to **Mocks** > **Get More Mock Packs**, or go directly to [preppeo.com/mocks/tokens](https://preppeo.com/mocks/tokens).

![Token store page](placeholder-token-store.png)

---

## Step 2: Choose a Pack

Select the pack that fits your needs:

| Pack | Price | Per Mock |
|------|-------|----------|
| 1 Mock | ₹499 | ₹499 |
| 3 Mocks | ₹999 | ₹333 |
| 5 Mocks | ₹1,499 | ₹300 |

![Pack selection](placeholder-pack-cards.png)

---

## Step 3: Apply a Referral Code (Optional)

If you have a partner or referral code, enter it before checkout to get a discount.

![Referral code input](placeholder-referral-input.png)

---

## Step 4: Complete Payment

Click **Buy Now** to proceed to Razorpay checkout. You can pay with:
- UPI
- Credit/Debit Card
- Net Banking
- Wallets

![Razorpay checkout](placeholder-razorpay-checkout.png)

---

## Step 5: Receive Your Access Codes

After payment, your access codes are:
1. Displayed on screen immediately
2. Sent to your registered email

![Access codes received](placeholder-codes-received.png)

Each code is unique and can be used once. You can share codes with others — they work regardless of who uses them.
