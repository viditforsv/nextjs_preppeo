# Managing Your Profile

Update your name, avatar, and account details from the profile page.

---

## Accessing Your Profile

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner of the header.
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.

![Profile menu item](placeholder-profile-menu.png)

---

## Update Your Name

1. On the profile page, edit your **First Name** and **Last Name** fields.
2. Click **Save** to update.

![Edit name fields](placeholder-edit-name.png)

---

## Change Your Avatar

1. Click on your current avatar or the placeholder icon.
2. Choose from the available avatar options or upload a custom image.
3. Click **Save** to confirm.

![Avatar picker](placeholder-avatar-picker.png)

---

## Email Address

Your email is set during sign-up and is used for:
- Login
- Receiving mock access codes after purchase
- Score report notifications

To change your email, contact support at contact@preppeo.com.

---

## Password

If you signed up with email and password:
1. Use the **Forgot Password** flow on the login page to reset it.
2. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

If you signed up with Google, no password is needed — you always log in via Google.
