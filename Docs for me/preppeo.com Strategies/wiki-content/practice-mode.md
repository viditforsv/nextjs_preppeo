# Practice Mode

Practice Mode lets you drill SAT questions by topic and difficulty — without taking a full mock.

---

## How It Works

1. Go to [preppeo.com/sat-test](https://preppeo.com/sat-test) and select **Practice Mode**.

![Practice mode selection](placeholder-practice-mode-select.png)

2. Choose your section (Math or Reading & Writing).

3. Optionally filter by domain (e.g., Algebra, Geometry) and difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard).

![Practice configuration](placeholder-practice-config.png)

4. Questions are served one at a time with instant feedback and explanations.

![Practice question view](placeholder-practice-question.png)

---

## Free Daily Limit

Every user gets 5 free practice questions per day:
- 2 Easy
- 2 Medium
- 1 Hard

The limit resets daily. A usage indicator shows how many questions you have left.

![Daily usage indicator](placeholder-daily-usage.png)
