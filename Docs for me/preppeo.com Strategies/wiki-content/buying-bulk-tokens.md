## How to Purchase

1. Go to the Token Store at [preppeo.com/mocks/tokens](https://preppeo.com/mocks/tokens).
2. Select an institute pack.
3. Complete payment via Razorpay (UPI, card, net banking).

![Bulk purchase flow](placeholder-bulk-purchase.png)

4. After payment, all access codes are:
   - Displayed on screen
   - Sent to your registered email

---

## Distributing Tokens to Students

Each access code is a unique string (e.g., SAT-X7KM-9RPQ). You can:
- Share codes individually with students via WhatsApp, email, or in class.
- Print a list of codes and hand them out.
- Each code works once, regardless of who uses it.

![Access codes list](placeholder-codes-list.png)
