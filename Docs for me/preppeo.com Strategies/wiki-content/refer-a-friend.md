# Refer a Friend

Share Preppeo with friends and both of you earn a free mock token.

---

## How the Referral Program Works

1. You share your unique referral link.
2. Your friend signs up and claims their free mock using your link.
3. Both you and your friend receive a **bonus mock token**.

---

## Step 1: Get Your Referral Link

1. Log in and click your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Refer & Earn** from the dropdown.

![Refer & Earn menu item](placeholder-refer-menu.png)

3. Your referral page shows your unique link and referral code.

![Referral page with link](placeholder-referral-page.png)

---

## Step 2: Share the Link

Click **Copy Referral Link** and share it via WhatsApp, email, or any messaging app. The link takes your friend directly to the free mock claim page with your code pre-filled.

![Copy referral link](placeholder-copy-link.png)

---

## Step 3: Track Your Referrals

The referral page shows how many friends have signed up using your link.

![Referral stats](placeholder-referral-stats.png)

---

## Rules

- Both the referrer and the referred friend must have a Preppeo account.
- Self-referrals are not allowed (you can't use your own code).
- Each friend-pair earns the bonus once — referring the same person again won't generate additional tokens.
- Bonus tokens are for the SAT exam by default.
